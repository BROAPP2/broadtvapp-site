Account Deletion Request

Manage your account and personal data

If you wish to delete your AgroTech TV account and all associated data, please follow the steps below. We will process your request within 7 business days.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to gerencia@softcomputo.com.co with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request – AgroTech TV"
  2. Include in the body of the email:
    • Your registered email address
    • Your full name (as registered in the app)
    • Reason for deletion (optional)
  3. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

What data is deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account credentials (email and password)
  • Your viewing history and activity logs
  • Any profile information associated with your account

Data retention periods

Some data may be retained for up to 30 days for backup and security audit purposes before permanent deletion. After this period, all data is fully and irreversibly removed from our systems.

Subscription & billing

If you have an active subscription, please note that deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription. Contact us to cancel any active billing before requesting deletion.
